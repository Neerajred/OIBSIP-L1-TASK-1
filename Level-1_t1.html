Telangana is a state in south-central India that was formed in June 2014 by splitting from Andhra Pradesh123. It has an area of about 114,840 square km and a population of about 35.8 million1. It is bordered by Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh, Odisha, Andhra Pradesh, and Karnataka345. Its capital is Hyderabad, which is also the capital of Andhra Pradesh for 10 years124. It has a history of being ruled by the nizams and the British before joining Andhra Pradesh in 195614. It is composed of mostly gneissic rock and has two plateaus: the Telangana Plateau and the Golconda Plateau.
